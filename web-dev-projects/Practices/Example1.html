Welcome to the Cats shelter

Welcome to our Cat Shelter! Our mission is to provide a safe and loving home for cats in need. Whether you're looking to adopt a new feline friend or simply want to support our cause, you've come to the right place.

Adoption Requirements for Cats

Main Requirements:

  • Applicant must be at least 18 years old.
  • Demonstrate the ability to provide proper care, including food, water, and veterinary care.
  • Because cats are unique:
    • Ability to interpret mysterious meows is a plus!
    • Acceptance of the occasional 'gift' of a half-eaten toy mouse.
